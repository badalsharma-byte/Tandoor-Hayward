NEW OWNERSHIP

A FRESH START

SAME LOCATION

Under new management, we're bringing back what Tandoor was always meant to be — authentic Indian cuisine made fresh, from scratch, every single day.

I. A NEW BEGINNING

When we took over Tandoor Indian Restaurant, we made a promise: to restore the quality and authenticity that this location deserves. The previous ownership had let standards slip, but we saw the potential — a beloved neighborhood spot waiting to be revived.

Today, everything is different. Our kitchen operates with one simple rule: fresh, every day. We don't use pre-packaged ingredients or frozen shortcuts. Our chicken is cut fresh daily. Our spices are ground in-house. Our bread comes straight from the tandoor to your table. This is Indian cooking the way it should be.

II

FRESH INGREDIENTS,
NO COMPROMISES

We believe great food starts with great ingredients. That's why we source fresh produce daily, cut our chicken fresh every morning, grind our own spice blends, and prepare everything from scratch. No frozen, no pre-made, no shortcuts — just honest, authentic Indian cooking.
